Our Story

Born on the trail,
built for the outdoors.

GearBox started in 2016 as a small Belgrade shop run by a group of passionate hikers and trail runners who were tired of overpriced, low-quality gear with no real expertise behind it. We wanted a place where every product recommendation came from actual experience — not a sales target.

We partnered directly with the brands we trusted on our own expeditions: Salomon for the trails, The North Face and Naturehike for the mountains, Osprey for the long hauls, and Garmin for navigation when the path runs out. Every item in our catalogue has been carried, worn or used by someone on our team.

Today we ship across the region and help thousands of adventurers — from weekend campers to multi-day trekkers — find gear that genuinely matches their ambitions.
