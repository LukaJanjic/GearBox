Our Story

Built by car people,
for car people.

GearBox was founded in 2012 by a group of automotive engineers who were frustrated by the lack of a reliable, transparent source for quality car parts in the region. What started as a small workshop supply operation has grown into a full-scale e-commerce platform serving thousands of customers across the Balkans.

We believe every driver deserves access to genuine, performance-grade components — regardless of whether they're maintaining a daily commuter or building a track-day machine. Our catalogue spans everything from OEM replacement parts to high-performance upgrades across all major vehicle brands.

Today, GearBox is trusted by over 500 independent workshops and tens of thousands of car enthusiasts who rely on us for speed, accuracy, and expertise.
